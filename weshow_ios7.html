Background Information

Weshow was both a mobile video editing tool and a social network application. One year after the first version of Weshow could be downloaded from APP store, the iOS7 was launched and the trend of visual design has been changed dramatically-- the Flat Design had become dominant in the internet industry. Thus, to follow the trend, the existing APP WeShow had to be overhauled and redesigned according to the brand new official guidance of iOS7. As the competitors were doing the same kind of redesign, to ensure the product being competitive, the work had to been done within 10 weeks, which is not only a challenge for the development team but also means there was little time left for designers. Fortunately, the project has been completed timely.

Keywords

Since the launch of iOS7 was a big event in the industry that the overhaul was necessary to keep the product being competitive in the market. And replacing all the widgets was already a huge task while there was left little time for the team to complete the overhaul timely, as the first version after the launch, the program manager decided the goal of the redesign basically is to change the whole product into the new style following all the principles and rules of the new operation system. Therefore, without too much struggling, after discussion, the product manager, the designers and the developers reached an agreement to define the keywords of the redesign as “Simple”, “Light” and “Elegant”.

Change of the Design

According to the new iOS guidance, not only the visual design had been overhauled, but also the interaction design experienced a huge change. The whole application seemed totally different after a short time period.

Homepage

Weshow was an application on cell-phones which both a video-editing tool and a social network product. Since the homepage of Weshow basically was the timeline of all the feeds released by people who the users followed, which included a great amount of information, I want to take this page for example to explain the details of the redesign. Different from realistic design—the style of the former version-- which could use various textures to divide different functional areas, by practicing Flat Design the abundant content of the homepage had become a challenge for us. Thus we classified all and the information into several groups to simplify the problem, thus by elaborately design all the elements -- colors, lines, font size, icons, white space, we solved the problem successfully.

Main pages

Since there were only 8 visual designers in the team, and while we were exploring the new version, some people had to work on the daily operation and details optimizing of the existing product. Thus, after the homepage was finalized, I arranged three major designers spent a week working out the main pages.

Guidance of Visual Design

Since the whole design had been changed, we summarized all the details—color, widgets, icons, and so forth into guidance. This not only clarified all the changes but also guaranteed a smooth teamwork.
