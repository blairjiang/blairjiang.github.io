About Me

I’m a UX designer who is currently studying Interaction Design at Carnegie Mellon University. I have an academic background in Industrial Design and Digital Art (Animation) and several year’s work experience as a UX designer in the Internet industry. For me, design is a powerful tool which can be used as both a telescope and a microscope, enabling designers to the parts of a system as well as the relationship between them.

Work Experience

As a Design Lead at Tencent, China’s leading Internet company, I was at the forefront of the rapidly developing Internet industry in China. Here, I got to work with outstanding user experience designers, who constantly inspired and helped me advance my professional ability. I designed Internet products ranging from software on personal computer to websites and mobile applications for iOS and Android platforms.
